Making something from nothing

Ever wanted to know how to make something from nothing? This is how you accomplish that.

All you will need is...

  • A lot of nothing
  • A magic wand
  • A cauldron
  • A pointed hat(preferably with a yellow star in the center)
  • Magic words

Getting started

  1. Place the pointed hat on your head
  2. Place all of your nothing in the cauldron
  3. Wave your wand over the cauldron and say the magic words

If done correctly, you should now have a bunch of something in your cauldron! :D
