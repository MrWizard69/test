The Soup Making Process

If you have ever wanted to make soup from a can, then this page is just for you!

The things you will need...

  • A can of your flavorite soup.
  • A can opener of sorts.
  • A bowl
  • A microwave
  • And a smile on your face :D

Warming the soup

Now that you have everything you need, let's put these ingredients together in a meaningful way.

  1. First off, take can opener and open the can of soup.
  2. Take open can and pour it into bowl.
  3. Take bowl of cold soup and place into the microwave.
  4. Turn microwave on and leave it on for a little while.
  5. Take soup out of microwave. Careful, contents may be hot!
  6. Finally, find your smile and put it on your face! :D
